Angel Saldana

Angel Saldana

Character Art & Game Design

Angel is an emerging character artist working in San Antonio, Texas.

He works primarily in digital media with the occasional paper and pencil sketch to brainstorm ideas. He mainly employs influences from high fantasy, traditional cultures and tabletop roleplays.

With a deep passion for lore and heritage found in our world, Angel is an ideal candidate for games and comics that strive to create an original, captivating world.
